About Tyflex

Tyflex is a Zimbabwean technology group operating across two pillars: Tyflex Solutions — delivering enterprise technology products and services, and Tyflex Ventures — building and scaling technology companies that serve African markets.

Our Story

Founded over a decade ago in Harare, Tyflex started as an IT solutions provider serving small and medium businesses. Today, we’ve grown into a technology group that not only delivers enterprise solutions but also builds our own technology ventures.

Our Solutions division is a trusted partner for UCaaS, networking, POS systems, barcode solutions, enterprise printing, and ERP software. Our Ventures division has launched companies like Vekta (logistics), Omniflex (messaging), and Mediaserv Digital (digital publishing & events, home of MineCon — Zimbabwe’s premier mining & construction exhibition).

Our Mission

To empower Zimbabwean businesses with world-class technology that drives efficiency, growth, and competitiveness in the modern economy.

Our Vision

To be Zimbabwe’s most trusted technology partner — building, deploying, and scaling solutions that transform how African businesses operate.
