Our Ventures

Beyond solutions, Tyflex builds and scales technology companies that serve African markets. Each venture tackles a critical gap in the Zimbabwean digital economy.

Vekta

Last-Mile Delivery & Digital Commerce

Vekta is an integrated logistics and commerce platform powering last-mile delivery for businesses and consumers across Zimbabwe. We connect merchants with riders and customers through a seamless digital experience.

From on-demand deliveries to scheduled dispatches, Vekta is building the infrastructure for e-commerce fulfilment in Zimbabwe.

Omniflex

AI-Powered Bulk Messaging Platform

Omniflex is our enterprise messaging platform delivering millions of SMS and enabling businesses to reach their customers at scale. With smart automation, delivery analytics, and API integrations, Omniflex powers the Bulk Messaging solution offered by Tyflex Solutions.

Built for the African market, Omniflex supports all local networks with competitive per-message pricing and 99%+ delivery rates.

Mediaserv Digital

Digital Publishing & Event Technology

Mediaserv Digital is our digital media company powering online publications, event management platforms, and content distribution across Zimbabwe. We combine technology with media to create impactful digital experiences.

Mediaserv Digital is the home of MineCon — Zimbabwe’s premier mining & construction exhibition, connecting industry leaders, suppliers, and innovators in the resource sector.
